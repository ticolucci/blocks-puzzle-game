# Stories

## 1. Bomb piece
The bomb piece, if we put it on the board, when there are adjacent pieces, it clears them all in a radius of 2 cells (configured).
the bomb piece appears after placing 3 red pieces.
the bomb piece is a 1x1 piece grey with a bomb icon on it.

## 2. Rainbow pieces
once in a while, a rainbow piece appears in the selector. The rainbow piece can be placed anywhere on the board. and when placed, a nan nan cat pass by the screen.

## 4. Bonus points when the grid gets completely empty
